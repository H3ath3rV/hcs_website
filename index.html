Our Core Values

Raising Decision Makers, World Shakers & Challenge Takers

At His Church School, we focus on developing the whole child through our commitment to excellence in education, spiritual growth, and character formation.

Academic Excellence

Our CAPS-aligned curriculum prepares students for the National Senior Certificate while integrating a biblical worldview into every subject.

Spiritual Formation

Daily devotionals, weekly chapel services, and discipleship groups help students develop a personal relationship with God.

Character Development

We instill integrity, respect, responsibility, and compassion through community service and leadership opportunities.

Family Partnership

We partner with families to build a supportive, faith-filled community where children thrive spiritually, academically, and socially through shared values and encouragement.

Cultural Diversity

We embrace students from diverse backgrounds and celebrate each child's unique God-given identity and potential.

Individual Attention

Small class sizes allow our dedicated teachers to provide personalized learning experiences for every student.
